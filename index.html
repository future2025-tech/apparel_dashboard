How to Recycle

Follow these steps to properly recycle your EcoWear t-shirt and minimize environmental impact:

Prepare the Garment

Ensure the t-shirt is clean and dry. Remove any non-fabric components like buttons or zippers if possible.

Locate Recycling Facilities

Use online search engines or local directories to find recycling facilities that accept textile waste in your area.

Understand Recycling Symbols

Familiarize yourself with recycling symbols to ensure proper sorting and disposal. Look for symbols indicating textile recycling or specific material types.
